Who are we?

We are a small website that aims to provide free religious and historical education for all visitors. Currently we are focusing specifically on the Seerah but we one aim to create a parent site that will include a diverse amount of content suitable for all visitors.

Why we created this site?

We created this site because in our experiences religion was something we were just told to do and when we asked questions we rarely every got our questions answered. Thus, we decided to first create a site that focuses on our religion Islam at the begining. Our goal is that kids and adults who are confused and/or not sure of Islam and the Prophet can find the answers they are looking for. Of course we have tried to make our site as unbiased as possible as in not to push our views on the readers.
