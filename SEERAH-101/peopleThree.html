Names Mentioned

Aminah: Mother of the prophet.

Umm Ayman: Muslim African woman described by Muhammad as his "mother after my own mother. She is the rest of my family”. Originally a house servant for Muhammads parents her freedom was eventually granted by Muhammad.

Muhammad: The future prophet. PBUH

Abd al-Muttalib: Father of Abdullah, Grandfather of Muhammad

Abu Talib: Brother of Abdullah and son of Abd al-Muttalib the one who took in Muhammad after Abd al-Mutalibs death and defended him in prophethood. He was responsible for Food and Beverages of Hajj pilgrims.

Fatimah bint Asad: Wife of Abu Talib and one of the earliest supporters of prophet Muhammad. She would be the mother of some of prophet Muhammads closets companions.
