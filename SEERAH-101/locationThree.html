Places/Items Mentioned

Kazraji (Banu Khazraj): A large tribe in Medina, Saudi Arabia. They were also in Medina during Muhammad's era.

Abwa: The place where Muhammad's mother, Aminah bint Wahb, died.

Mecca: The holiest city in Islam and the capital of the Makkah Province of Saudi Arabia.

Yathrib: Modern Medina.

Hijr: A low wall originally part of the Kaaba.
